Hey there,

It's 2021 and African creatives are still struggling.

For years, African creatives have been victims of the inability to earn what our work is worth. Somehow, our society has managed to create a dynamic where the creator, the mastermind and the one with the actual talent remains at the bottom of the food chain.

Sadly, many of us have been forced to fit into boxes that were made to keep us from achieving true creative freedom. We have to present and/or sell our work through organizations and platforms that most times act as gatekeepers and rob us of authenticity, ownership and reward.

Our time is now.

With Creathor, we put ourselves in the shoes of every kind of creative out there. Be it as a blogger, writer, filmmaker, artist etc., we have gone back to the basics and have put together a suite of powerful tools for independent creators to create, distribute and monetise their best work with ease.

Creathor is your stand for creative freedom, and we hope that you stand with us too.

Philip Obosi,
For the Creathor Team.
